Why Blood Group Awareness Matters

Understanding genetics isn't just about prediction; it's about preparation. Beyond the fascinating science of whether a child inherits A, B, or O alleles, identifying the Rh factor is critical for a smooth pregnancy.

What is Rh Incompatibility?

If a mother is Rh-negative and the baby inherits an Rh-positive gene from the father, the mother's immune system might produce antibodies against the baby's blood. Early awareness ensures proper medical intervention, neutralizing risks.
